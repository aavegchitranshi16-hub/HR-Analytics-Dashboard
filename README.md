# HR-Analytics-Dashboard

👥 **HR Analytics Dashboard: Workforce Insights & Salary Analysis**
An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze employee distribution, salary trends, and hiring patterns—helping HR teams make data-driven workforce decisions.

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** provides a comprehensive overview of employee demographics, salary distribution, and hiring trends. It enables HR professionals and business leaders to monitor workforce composition, identify salary patterns, and optimize hiring strategies.

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Core tool used for building interactive visuals and reports
📂 **Power Query** – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and shaping
🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Created measures like Average Salary, Total Employees, and dynamic aggregations
📝 **Data Modeling** – Relationships between employee attributes (Gender, Job Title, Date of Join, Salary) for cross-filtering
📁 **File Formats** – .pbix (development), .png (presentation snapshot)
    **Data Source** - Source: Internal HR dataset (simulated/sample data)

**Business Problem**

**Organizations often struggle to:**
Understand workforce composition (gender diversity, age groups)
Analyze salary distribution across roles and experience levels
Track hiring trends over time
Identify gaps in compensation and workforce planning

**Goal of the Dashboard**

**To build an interactive tool that:**
Provides a clear snapshot of workforce metrics
Enables HR teams to analyze salary distribution and employee demographics
Tracks hiring trends month-over-month
Supports strategic decisions in hiring, compensation, and diversity

**Business Impact & Insights**
📈 Workforce Planning: Identify hiring trends and plan recruitment cycles effectively
💰 Compensation Strategy: Understand salary distribution across roles and age groups
⚖️ Diversity Insights: Monitor gender balance and improve inclusivity initiatives
🎯 Role-Based Analysis: Helps HR identify which roles drive higher salary costs
📊 Data-Driven Decisions: Leadership can use insights for budgeting, hiring, and promotions
